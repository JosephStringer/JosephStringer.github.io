Caravan Palace is an electro swing band based in Paris. Originally formed in 2008, they debuted with their studio album titled "Caravan Palace". The band was orignally made up of three members, however, over the time from 2006 to 2008 the band gained more members and became the band it is today. Many of Caravan Palace's songs take inspiration from old music from the 50's and the surrounding era, taking samples from certain songs, so that they can be used to create new music.

The current members of caravan palace shown in the photo from left to right are: Antoine Toustou (Guitar), Charles Delaporte (Double Bass), Arnaud Vial (Guitar), Zoé Colotis (Vocals), Hughes Payen (Violin), Paul-Marie Barbier (Percussion), Camille Chapelière (Clarinet).

Since debuting, Caravan Palace has released two more sudio albums, Panic and <Io_oI>, along with two EPs, and two non-album singles.
