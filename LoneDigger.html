Video

Lone Digger is the first song on the group's third studio album, <Io_oI>, and recieved it's own music video on the 12th of November 2015.

Since it's release, the music video has gained more than 110 million views on YouTube alone and has won awards such as the 'Top Shorts 2017' with honorable mention, as well as being shown and being runner up at many film and music festivals, due to help from it's striking visuals and surreal imagery

The video was produced with direction from the team at Double Ninja, a Paris based directing team comprised of Thomas Vernay and Yann Wallaert. Aswell as producing Lone Digger, Double Ninja has produced award winning music videos for the bands 'Kids and Explosions' and 'Mystery Skulls'.
